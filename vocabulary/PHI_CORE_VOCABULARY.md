## TOTAL: 957 Core Words

This list provides a solid foundation of ~960 words that are:
- Cross-culturally universal (based on Swadesh list and linguistic research)
- Immediately useful for basic communication
- Appropriate for an analytical language structure
- Balanced between different semantic categories

Note: Some culturally specific items (like specific tools or social roles) are included because they represent universal human concepts, even if the specific forms vary by culture.
