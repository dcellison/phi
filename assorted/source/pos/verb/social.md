# social

> This vocabulary represents fundamental human experiences of interaction, cooperation, community building, and relationship formation found universally across contemporary global cultures. These concepts encompass the full spectrum of social behavior, from basic interpersonal communication through complex group dynamics, collective decision-making, and community governance that define human social existence. The verbs included here address core social phenomena: forming relationships, building trust, sharing resources, resolving conflicts, establishing hierarchies, maintaining traditions, expressing solidarity, and navigating social roles within communities. Each verb captures essential aspects of human social experience including bonding, cooperation, competition, communication, ritual participation, mutual aid, social learning, cultural transmission, and the intricate web of reciprocal relationships that enable human societies to function, flourish, and adapt across diverse environments and challenges throughout our species' history.
