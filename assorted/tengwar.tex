\section{Introduction}
\textsc{Phi} is a constructed language. Its name embodies the simplicity and softness of the language itself. \textit{Phi} ($\varPhi$) is also beauty in mathematics. It is
\begin{quote}
    ``\emph{the golden ratio, an irrational number with a value of approximately 1.618033988 which expresses the relationship that the sum of two quantities is to the larger quantity as the larger is to the smaller.}'' - Wiktionary
\end{quote}

It is thought that the value of \textit{phi} is one of the fundamental characteristics of nature; in the language Phi, nature is fundamental to its being. Listening to spoken Phi, one can be reminded of the sounds of the natural world. There is also a musicality and rhythm to sentences in the language.
