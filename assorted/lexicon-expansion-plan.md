## systematic creation process

### step 1: semantic mapping
1. identify universal concepts across cultures
2. group by cognitive/conceptual categories
3. prioritize by frequency of use

### step 2: phonotactic validation
1. apply correct pattern for part of speech
2. ensure no duplicate syllables within word
3. verify phonological distinctiveness from existing words

### step 3: semantic field integration
1. ensure consistency within semantic domains
2. maintain systematic relationships
3. avoid cultural bias or specificity

### step 4: systematic testing
1. create example sentences using new words
2. test in multiple grammatical contexts
3. verify pattern recognition accuracy
