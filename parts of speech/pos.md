noun (n.)
names persons, places, things, ideas, or qualities
boy river shoreline desk fear happiness

pronoun (pron.)
usually substitutes for nouns and functions as nouns
i you he she it we they myself this that who which everyone

verb (v.)
expresses actions, occurrences, or states of being
be become bunt inflate run talk

adverb (adv.)
describes or modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs
almost gently helpfully someday

adjective (adj.)
describes or modifies nouns or pronouns
gentle helpful small red round

preposition (prep.)
relates nouns or pronouns to other words in a sentence
about at down for of with

conjunction (conj.)
links words, clauses, and phrases
for and nor but or yet so after as how if

determiner (det.)
specifies and identifies nouns
these that those enough much few which what

interjection (interj.)
expresses feeling or commands attention, either alone or in a sentence
darn hey oh wow

object (obj.)
