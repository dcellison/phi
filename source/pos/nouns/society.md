# people and relationships

*core human social connections*

> This vocabulary represents a revolutionary transformation of human social categorization through peace linguistics principles and modern practical universality. Unlike standard linguistic inventories, these concepts actively promote peaceful thinking, inclusive connection, and collaborative relationships across contemporary global cultures. Each category has been carefully curated to eliminate division-promoting language, hierarchical power structures, and exclusionary concepts. Instead, the terms emphasize shared humanity, mutual respect, collaborative learning, and peaceful community belonging. From inclusive identity categories that transcend binary thinking, to relationship terms that focus on connection rather than separation, to public roles that welcome rather than exclude, to exchange relationships that promote mutual learning over hierarchy - this collection embodies how language can consciously foster peace, understanding, and universal human dignity in our interconnected world.
