# consumables

*universal food and drink terminology for cross-cultural sustenance communication*

> this category represents universal food and drink concepts documented across human cultures through research in food anthropology, subsistence universals, and cross-linguistic nutrition studies. these 100 consumables are organized into seven nutritional domains that appear consistently across world food systems and culinary traditions: basic food categories, dairy and animal products, processed foods and ingredients, seasonings and flavorings, beverages, prepared foods, and preparation states.
