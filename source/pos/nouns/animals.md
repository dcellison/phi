# animals

*core animal concepts for human interaction*

> this category represents universal animal concepts documented across human cultures through research in zoological anthropology, ethnobiology, and cross-cultural animal classification systems. these 104 animals are organized into eight domains that appear consistently across world taxonomic systems and cultural traditions: general terms, domestic and farm animals, wild mammals, birds, reptiles and amphibians, insects and arthropods, aquatic and marine animals, and other invertebrates.
