# natural places

*fundamental geographical and natural locations*

> this category represents universal natural places that unite contemporary global cultures in shared wonder and peaceful connection to diverse landscapes. applying peace linguistics principles, these places celebrate both dramatic natural phenomena that inspire universal awe and accessible refuges that provide peace and renewal. organized into six domains of meaningful landscape relationship: elevated places that inspire and shelter, life-giving waters that sustain and heal, dynamic shores where land meets sea, sheltered valleys and geological wonders, vast open spaces that expand horizons, and ancient forests that serve as natural sanctuaries. these selections honor the full spectrum of how humanity experiences place - from dramatic wilderness that moves the spirit to peaceful refuges that restore the soul, fostering appreciation for our shared planetary home.
