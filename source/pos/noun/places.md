# places

*peaceful spatial relationships and connections*

> This vocabulary represents a revolutionary transformation of spatial categorization through peace linguistics principles and indigenous spatial concepts. Unlike Western hierarchical organization of space, these categories emphasize peaceful relationships with place, connection over division, and respectful interaction with natural and built environments. The structure reflects universal human spatial relationships found across contemporary global cultures while eliminating colonial concepts of ownership, control, and dominance. Each category promotes collaborative coexistence with land and community rather than hierarchical development or resource extraction.
