# time concepts

*fundamental temporal understanding*

> This vocabulary represents a revolutionary transformation of temporal categorization through peace linguistics principles and modern practical universality. Unlike Western hierarchical organization of time concepts, these categories emphasize peaceful relationships with temporality, honoring both individual and community experiences across contemporary global cultures. The structure reflects universal human temporal relationships found across all documented cultures while eliminating colonial temporal hierarchies. Each category promotes inclusive coexistence of different temporal understandings, welcoming linear, cyclical, and relational approaches to time. The concepts included here celebrate shared human experiences of growth, natural rhythms, and community connection across diverse contemporary global cultures.
