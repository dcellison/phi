# body parts

*fundamental human anatomy for universal communication*

> this category represents universal anatomical concepts documented across human cultures through research in medical anthropology, cross-linguistic anatomy studies, and ethnobiological classification systems. these 77 body parts are organized into eight anatomical domains that appear consistently across world languages and medical traditions: head and face, mouth and oral cavity, upper extremities, lower extremities, torso and trunk, internal organs, reproductive organs, and body materials and substances.
