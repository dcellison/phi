# people and relationships

*core human social connections*

> This vocabulary represents a revolutionary transformation of human social categorization through peace linguistics principles and earth-connected wisdom. Unlike standard linguistic inventories that emphasize separation and hierarchy, these concepts actively promote harmonious thinking, reciprocal relationships, and collaborative stewardship across all aspects of community life. Each category has been carefully curated to eliminate division-promoting language, extractive practices, and domination-based concepts. Instead, the terms emphasize shared humanity, mutual respect, collaborative learning, earth partnership, and peaceful community belonging that honors both human relationships and our sacred connections to the natural world.
>
> From inclusive identity categories that transcend binary thinking, to relationship terms that focus on connection rather than separation, to earth-stewardship roles that embody reciprocal care rather than extraction, to traditional craft knowledge that celebrates community wisdom over individual ownership - this collection embodies how language can consciously foster peace, understanding, ecological harmony, and universal dignity in our interconnected world. The vocabulary particularly emphasizes earth-connected practices, seasonal wisdom, water stewardship, and agroforestry relationships that represent humanity's deeper, more harmonious past - a time of community-centered living, sustainable resource management, and respectful partnership with the natural world.
>
> These concepts resist the language of violence, competition, and environmental destruction, instead offering pathways toward the collaborative, earth-honoring, community-supported way of life that Phi envisions for humanity's future through remembering our wiser past.
