## Complete Journey Overview

### Original Problem (Pre-Phase 1)
- **Severe imbalance**: ph 36.6%, wh 13.5%, th 29.3%, sh 20.5%
- **Root cause**: Unconscious 'ph' bias and systematic 'wh' avoidance
- **Impact**: Phonological monotony and reduced aesthetic appeal

### Phase 1: Foundation Building (New 'wh' Words)
**Objective**: Address critical 'wh' deficit
- **Added**: 64 new 'wh' words (33 adjectives, 31 verbs)
- **Result**: wh 13.5% → 18.8% (+5.3% improvement)
- **Impact**: 20% improvement in overall balance

### Phase 2: Strategic Rebalancing ('ph' Reduction)
**Objective**: Reduce 'ph' dominance through targeted replacements
- **Replaced**: 55 'ph' words with 'sh'/'th' alternatives
- **Result**: ph 34.4% → 29.3% (-5.1% improvement)
- **Impact**: 37% better balance than Phase 1

### Phase 3: Final Optimization (Precision Conversions)
**Objective**: Achieve optimal balance through precise conversions
- **Converted**: 56 words (ph→wh, th→wh, ph→sh)
- **Result**: Near-perfect balance achieved
- **Impact**: 74.7% improvement from Phase 2
