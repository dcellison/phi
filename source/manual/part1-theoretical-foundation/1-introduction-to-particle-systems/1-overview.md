## Key Concepts

### Particles as Grammatical Tools

Particles are **independent words** that carry specific grammatical meanings, serving as the primary mechanism for expressing relationships between content words.

### Phi's Analytical Nature

Unlike synthetic languages that modify word endings, phi uses separate particles to express:
- Grammatical relationships (subject, object, verb marking)
- Temporal information (tense, aspect, mood)
- Evidential information (source of knowledge)
- Social relationships (politeness, formality)
- Discourse structure (topic, contrast, emphasis)

### Universal Accessibility

Particle systems exist across many human languages, making phi's approach cross-linguistically familiar and learnable.
