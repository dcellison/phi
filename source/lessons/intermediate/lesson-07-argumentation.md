# lesson 07: argumentation and logical reasoning

## logical relationship markers

phi organizes reasoning through explicit logical connectors:

### causal reasoning particles

| particle/conjunction | meaning | logical function |
|---------------------|---------|------------------|
| `renu` | because | direct causation |
| `tura` | since | given that/premise |
| `huwa` | so/therefore | conclusion |
| `wetu` | if | conditional premise |
| `mi` | however | counterargument |

### evidence and support markers

| particle | meaning | argumentative function |
|----------|---------|------------------------|
| `ha` | speaking of | topic introduction |
| `ma` | emphasis | highlighting key points |
| `hi` | direct evidence | "I observe that" |
| `ro` | inference | "I deduce that" |
| `nu` | hearsay | "others say that" |

## basic argument structure

### premise-conclusion pattern

**1. premise establishment**
```
ha  teshe wheisha, mia hi  phiato mo   pisha
TOP good  water,   me  DIR this   more soft
Speaking of good water, I observe this is softer
```

**2. reasoning development**
```
renu    phiato mo   pisha mia ro   mo   teshe phela
because this   more soft  me  INFR more good  feel
Because this is softer, I infer it feels better
```

**3. conclusion statement**
```
huwa mia phiso ha thi phiato wheisha phome
so   me   decide TOP you this   water  drink
Therefore, I decide that you drink this water
```

## evidence presentation

### direct observation arguments

```
mia hi  hashe nuthui mo   mipha nene hi  mo   teshe
me  DIR green pebble more beautiful and DIR more good
I directly observe green pebbles are more beautiful and better

renu    mia hi  phiato huwa mia phela ha  thi phamo
because me  DIR this   so   me  suggest TOP you like
Because I observe this, I suggest that you like them
```

### inferential reasoning

```
mia phose ha  thephoa mo   tapine phola
me  see   TOP person  more quickly walk
I see that the person walks more quickly

mia ro   thephoa teshe wheisha phano renu    mo   tapine
me  INFR person  good  water   get   because more quickly
I infer the person gets good water because they're quicker

huwa mia phela ha  thi thephoa phelu
so   me  suggest TOP you person  follow
Therefore, I suggest that you follow the person
```

### reported evidence and testimony

```
thephoa li  nu   ha  hashe siwhea mo   teshe
person  PST HRSY TOP green house  more good
The person reportedly said that green houses are better

mi   mia me  shule renu    mia me  hi  phiato
CNTR me  NEG understand because me  NEG DIR this
However, I don't understand because I don't observe this

huwa mia thisa hamite thi phemo ha  phiato
so   me  ask   how    you think TOP this
So I ask how you think about this
```

## counter-arguments and rebuttals

### systematic disagreement

**position a:**
```
mia phela ha  mipho siwhea mo   teshe
me  suggest TOP blue  house  more good
I suggest that blue houses are better
```

**position b (counter):**
```
mi   mia phemo ha  hashe siwhea mo   teshe renu    mia hi  hashe mo   pisha nene mo   mipha
CNTR me  think TOP green house  more good  because me  DIR green more soft  and  more beautiful
However, I think green houses are better because I observe green is softer and more beautiful
```

**position a (rebuttal):**
```
thi teshe shuso mi   mia hi  mipho mo   rithe nene mia ro   rithe mo   tapine huwa mo   teshe
you good  say   CNTR me  DIR blue  more near  and  me  INFR near  more quickly so   more good
You speak well, however I observe blue is nearer and I infer nearness is quicker, so better
```

## comparative evaluation

### systematic comparison framework

```
ha  mipho nene hashe siwhea, mia phela phiato:
TOP blue  and  green house,  me  suggest this:

lutane  mipho mo   mipha renu    mo   pisha riwhea
firstly blue  more beautiful because more soft  wind
First, blue is more beautiful because of softer wind

mi   hashe mo   teshe renu    mo   pisha wheisha
CNTR green more good  because more soft  water
However, green is better because of softer water

tumane  mia phiso ha  hashe pa   teshe renu    mo   pisha wheisha mo   thana mipha riwhea
finally me  decide TOP green most good  because more soft  water  more important beautiful wind
Finally, I decide green is best because softer water is more important than beautiful wind
```

### evidence weighing

```
mia phela phiato mo   thana renu:
me  suggest this   more important because:

phi - mia hi  wheisha teshe phela phera
one - me  DIR water   good  health be
One: I observe water provides good health

whu - mi   riwhea mo   mipha me  thana phela huwa mia phiso ha  teshe phela pa   thana
two - CNTR wind   more beautiful NEG important health so   me  decide TOP good  health most important
Two: However, beautiful wind doesn't provide important health, so I decide good health is most important
```

## hypothetical reasoning

### conditional argument chains

```
wetu mia hashe siwhea phamo mia su  mo   teshe phela
if   me  green house  like  me  FUT more good  feel
If I like green houses, I will feel better

nene wetu mia mo   teshe phela mia su  mo   tapine phuri
and  if   me  more good  feel  me  FUT more quickly work
And if I feel better, I will work more quickly

nene wetu mia mo   tapine phuri mia su  mo   teshe noshea phano
and  if   me  more quickly work  me  FUT more good  food   get
And if I work quicker, I will get better food

huwa wetu mia hashe siwhea phamo mia su  mo   teshe noshea phano
so   if   me  green house  like  me  FUT more good  food   get
Therefore, if I like green houses, I will get better food
```

### alternative scenario analysis

```
wetu mia mipho siwhea phamo mia su  hamite phela
if   me  blue  house  like  me  FUT how    feel
If I like blue houses, how will I feel?

mia ro   me  mo   teshe phela renu    mipho me  mo   pisha
me  INFR NEG more good  feel  because blue  NEG more soft
I infer I won't feel better because blue isn't softer

mi   wetu mia me  siwhea phamo mia su  hamite phela
CNTR if   me  NEG house  like  me  FUT how    feel
However, if I don't like houses, how will I feel?

mia ro   pa   pisha phela renu    siwhea me  teshe phela
me  INFR most soft  feel  because house  NEG good  health
I infer I'd feel worst because houses don't provide good health
```

## structured debate format

### opening statements

**debater a:**
```
ha  phiato thephoa, mia phela ha  hashe nuthui mo   teshe renu    mia hi  hashe mo   mipha nene mo   pisha
TOP this   debate,  me  suggest TOP green pebble more good  because me  DIR green more beautiful and  more soft
In this debate, I suggest that green pebbles are better because I observe green is more beautiful and softer
```

**debater b:**
```
mi   mia phela ha  mipho nuthui mo   teshe renu    mia hi  mipho mo   rithe siwhea nene mo   thana
CNTR me  suggest TOP blue  pebble more good  because me  DIR blue  more near  house  and  more important
However, I suggest blue pebbles are better because I observe blue is nearer to houses and more important
```

### evidence rounds

**debater a (evidence):**
```
mia phela phiato mo   thana renu:
me  suggest this   more important because:

phi - nu   thephoa li  shuso hashe mo   teshe phela
one - HRSY person  PST say   green more good  health
One: People reportedly said green provides better health

whu - mia hi  hashe nuthui mo   pisha whami phela
two - me  DIR green pebble more soft  rain  provide
Two: I observe green pebbles provide softer rain
```

**debater b (counter-evidence):**
```
thi teshe shuso mi:
you good  say   CNTR:

phi - mia ro   nu   me  hi  teshe phela renu    thephoa me  phemo
one - me  INFR HRSY NEG DIR good  health because person  NEG think
One: I infer hearsay isn't direct good health because people don't think

whu - mia hi  mipho nuthui mo   rithe huwa mo   tapine
two - me  DIR blue  pebble more near  so   more quickly
Two: I observe blue pebbles are nearer so quicker
```

### closing arguments

**debater a (synthesis):**
```
mia phemo ha  thi teshe shuso mi   mia phela:
me  think TOP you good  say   CNTR me  suggest:

wetu mo   tapine thana mia phamo mipho nuthui teshe
if   more quickly important me  like  blue  pebble good
If quickness is important, I agree blue pebbles are good

mi   wetu mo   teshe phela thana mia phamo hashe nuthui mo   teshe
CNTR if   more good  health important me  like  green pebble more good
However, if better health is important, I like green pebbles more

huwa mia phela ha  thi phemo hamite mo   thana
so   me  suggest TOP you think how    more important
Therefore, I suggest you think what's more important
```

## meta-argumentative discourse

### argument evaluation

```
ha  phiato thephoa thue phela, mia phemo:
TOP this   person  of   suggestion, me  think:

thephoa teshe phela renu    hi  teshe roma
person  good  suggest because DIR good  evidence
The person suggests well because of good direct evidence

mi   thephoa me  hi  shouphea huwa ro   me  mo   teshe
CNTR person  NEG DIR complete  so   INFR NEG more good
However, the person doesn't observe completely so inference isn't better

huwa mia phela ha  thephoa mo   shouphea roma phela
so   me  suggest TOP person  more complete evidence suggest
Therefore, I suggest the person provide more complete evidence
```

### discourse quality assessment

```
mia phemo ha  phiato phela mo   teshe renu:
me  think TOP this   argument more good  because:

lutane  shouphea roma phera - thi hi  nene ro   shuso
firstly complete evidence have - you DIR and  INFR say
First, it has complete evidence - you state both direct and inferential

tumane  thephoa teshe phelu renu    me  rashi phemi
finally person  good  listen because NEG angry speak
Finally, the person listens well because they don't speak angrily
```

## key insights from this lesson

1. **explicit logic**: phi makes reasoning relationships transparent through particles
2. **evidence hierarchies**: direct observation > inference > hearsay
3. **systematic rebuttals**: counter-arguments follow predictable patterns
4. **meta-discourse**: phi can discuss the quality of arguments themselves
5. **cultural neutrality**: logical structures work across all reasoning traditions
