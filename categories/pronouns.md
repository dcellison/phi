# pronouns

> pronouns replace nouns.

there are only three pronouns in phi: first, second, and third person. the particle `lo`  is used to indicate plurality, so where `mia` means `me` or `i`, `lo mia`, or `plural me`, means `us` or `we`. `lo sha` is used for `them` or `they`. other people are referred to as `it`, or `sha`, thus avoiding any pronoun issues. there are no gender specifications in phi. this is in keeping with the simplicity of an a priori pidgin.

| phi | eng | function
| --- | --- | --------
| mia | me  | 1st person
| thi | you | 2nd person
| sha | it  | 3rd person
