# example usage

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | we love the orange trees
| sov     | orange trees we love
| literal | (plural) me (object/plural) tree orange (verb) love
| plath   | (lo mia) (na lo lipha sathe) (te lothea)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | i love you
| sov     | i you love
| literal | me you (verb) love
| plath   | (mia) (thi) (te lothea)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | i love you (plural)
| sov     | i you love
| literal | me (plural) you (verb) love
| plath   | (mia) (lo thi) (te lothea)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | the sky is blue
| sov     | sky blue is
| literal | (subject) sky (object) blue (verb) is
| plath   | (si huhwa) (na mipho) (te phi)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | they like food
| sov     | they food like
| literal | (plural) it (object) food (verb) like
| plath   | (lo sha) (na nosheo) (te lephe)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | i like it
| sov     | i it like
| literal | me it (verb) like
| plath   | (mia) (sha) (te lephe)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | i do not like green
| sov     | i green do not like
| literal | me (object) green (verb/not) like
| plath   | (mia) (na hashe) (te ne lephe)

| type    | sentence
| ----    | --------
| english | i liked my friend
| sov     | i my friend liked
| encoded | me (object) friend of me (verb/past) like
| plath   | (mia) (si methu loa mia) (te li lephe)
