# example usage

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | we love the orange trees
| sov  | we orange trees love
| lit  | (plural) me (object/plural) tree orange (verb) love
| phi  | (lo mia) (na lo lipha sathe) (te lothea)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | i love you
| sov  | i you love
| lit  | me you (verb) love
| phi  | mia thi (te lothea)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | i love you (plural)
| sov  | i you love
| lit  | me (plural) you (verb) love
| phi  | mia (lo thi) (te lothea)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | the sky is blue
| sov  | sky blue is
| lit  | (subject) sky (object) blue (verb) is
| phi  | (si huhwa) (na mipho) (te phi)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | they like food
| sov  | they food like
| lit  | (plural) it (object) food (verb) like
| phi  | (lo sha) (na nosheo) (te lephe)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | i like it
| sov  | i it like
| lit  | me it (verb) like
| phi  | mia sha (te lephe)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | i do not like green
| sov  | i green do not like
| lit  | me (object) green (verb/not) like
| phi  | mia (na hashe) (te me lephe)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | i liked my friend
| sov  | i my friend liked
| lit  | me (object) friend of me (verb/past) like
| phi  | mia (si methu loa mia) (te li lephe)

| type | sentence
| ---- | --------
| eng  | the food is ours
| sov  | food ours is
| lit  | (subject) food of (object/plural) us (verb) is
