This vocabulary has been aligned with Phi's Five Pillars:
- solarpunk-values (sustainability, community, regeneration)
- secular-buddhist-philosophy (mindfulness, non-violence, interconnection)
- art-nouveau-aesthetics (organic beauty, natural forms)
- peace-linguistics-practices (non-violent communication, harmony)
- pre-industrial-wisdom (traditional knowledge, crafts, cycles)

All words are gender-neutral and violence has been removed or reframed.
