# φ

Phi is a constructed language. A constructed language is one whose phonology, grammar, and vocabulary, instead of having developed naturally, are consciously devised.<sup>[1](#note1)</sup> The sound of the name _Phi_ embodies the simplicity and softness of the language itself. _Phi (Φ)_ is also beauty in mathematics. it is

>"_the golden ratio, an irrational number with a value of approximately 1.618033988 which expresses the relationship that the sum of two quantities is to the larger quantity as the larger is to the smaller._" - Wiktionary<sup>[2](#note2)</sup>

&emsp;&ensp;It is thought that the value of _phi_ is one of the fundamental characteristics of nature; in the language Phi, nature is fundamental to its being. Listening to spoken Phi one is sometimes reminded of the sounds of the natural world. There is also a musicality and rhythm to sentences in the language.
