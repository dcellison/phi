# φ

the name _phi_ embodies the simplicity and softness of this language. _phi_ is also beauty in nature and mathematics. it is

>the golden ratio, an irrational number with a value of approximately 1.618033988 which expresses the relationship that the sum of two quantities is to the larger quantity as the larger is to the smaller. - [wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%CF%86)

it has been said that the value of phi is a fundamental characteristic of nature; in the language phi, nature is fundamental to its being. listening to spoken phi, one can be reminded of the sound of wind or water. in fact, the greek letter [`φ`](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voiceless_bilabial_fricative) is used to indicate that `ph` should be pronounced by expelling air through the lips instead of through the top teeth and bottom lip as happens with the sound of [`f`](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voiceless_labiodental_fricative). in effect you are creating a breeze when you're prononcing a word in phi that contains `ph`. there is also a musicality and rhythm to sentences in phi. the language also centers around nature-based concepts.
