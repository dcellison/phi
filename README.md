# φ

the name _phi_ embodies the simplicity and softness of this language. _phi_ is also beauty in nature and mathematics. it is

>the golden ratio, an irrational number with a value of approximately 1.618033988 which expresses the relationship that the sum of two quantities is to the larger quantity as the larger is to the smaller. - [wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%CF%86)

it has been said that phi is a fundamental characteristic of nature; in the language phi, nature is fundamental to its being. listening to spoken phi, one can be reminded of wind, water, and other naturally occuring sounds. in fact, the greek letter `φ` is used to indicate that `ph` should be pronounced by expelling air through one's lips, instead of with top teeth and bottom lip like the sound of `f`. in effect you're creating a breeze when you're prononcing a phi word that contains `ph`. there is also a musicality and rhythm to sentences in phi. the language also centers around nature-based concepts.
