## Implementation Notes

This expanded list now provides ~180-200 essential roots total, creating a robust foundation that:

1. **Covers basic communication needs** without relying on NSM primes
2. **Maintains philosophical alignment** with Phi's non-violent, mindful approach
3. **Provides compositional building blocks** for more complex concepts
4. **Remains culturally universal** while avoiding technology-specific or culture-bound terms
5. **Balances concrete and abstract** concepts for practical use

The roots are organized thematically but will need phonological forms assigned according to the sound-meaning correspondence system, ensuring that related concepts share phonological features where appropriate.
