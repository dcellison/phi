# Source material beside Phi

## A practice companion to the manual

Phi has a firm edge. Its sentences contain canonical vocabulary, ordinary grammar, transparent expressions, module terms, and valid Phi-form names. Foreign wording, exact records, other scripts, formulas, identifiers, legal clauses, medical data, citations, and quotations remain beside Phi in the surrounding medium.

This pamphlet practices four decisions: use Phi vocabulary, compose a clear expression, use a bearer-approved name after `ne`, or preserve a source artifact separately. It also practices translation without pretending that a translation is the source.
