# Part 3: Preserving records

An exact artifact stays exact by remaining where its medium can preserve it. A Markdown document may use a code span or block, an interface may display a field, a printed work may use a table or note, and a conversation may show, spell, cite, or otherwise establish the artifact before discussing it in Phi.

```
mia ha masue tukiro.
1SG PROX measure review.
(I review this measurement.)
```

The record preserves characters and provenance. Phi names the actions, relationships, evidence, uncertainty, authority, and consequences around it. Neither layer silently replaces the other.

What holds for a measurement holds for a person's words. Testimony that must stay exact stays in its own words and its own language, beside the Phi that says who spoke, what is at stake, and what should happen next.
