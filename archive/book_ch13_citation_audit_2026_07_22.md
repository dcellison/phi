# Book chapter 13 citation audit (2026-07-22)

This audit verifies every citation and load-bearing claim in `book/13_escaping_words.md` as merged in pull request #507. An independent verification pass read the finished chapter cold and checked its final wording, sentence by sentence, against the primary sources: Rowland's Tumblr post and her Stellar Beacon essay, the Collins shortlist blog and the archived Collins dictionary headword page, the archived Comrada translation of Čapek's 1933 column, the stored full text of the 1976 Selfish Gene first edition, the Drews and Antal record via Crossref and its abstract, and the Encyclopedia of Science Fiction entry. Every correction was applied to the chapter in the same pull request as this record.

## Method note

The one real defect repeated the series' most persistent failure shape in a new position: a provenance field composed from a verification agent's label rather than from the page itself. The pre-draft pass had verified the quotes on the archived Čapek page and called the page by an institutional name the page does not carry, and the name flowed into the reference unexamined. The chapter 12 lesson, that title lines need verification even when a page's quotes were pinned, extends one field further: publisher and venue fields need the same direct look, because they are precisely where a plausible label substitutes for a read. The audit's second finding was the same shape smaller, two venue names for one publication. Both corrections were verified against the stored captures before application, and no audit finding was overturned this round.
