# The emotions and inner life audit (2026-07-06)

The survey behind the *heart composes* ruling in `/canon.md`: the fourth of the seven system sweeps. No new words were coined and no carve lines were needed; the grammar was ruled in the body sweep, the carves in the synonym audit, and the one remaining gap was a coining stance that existed only as a single registry row's aside.

## What the survey found

**The felt basics are coined and correctly classed for the feeling grammar.** The noun feelings take `phaelo` exactly as the states-are-worn-sensations-are-felt ruling requires: `shea` (peace), `nuhe` (sadness), `sukima` (fear), `nupira` (shame, whose entry already releases it: `shia nupira to leiro.`), `thomari` (courage). The adjective states take `nai`: `siora` (joy), `loshi` (joy-giving), `shena` (calm), `phaelu` (peaceful), `noalu` (tranquil), `thiro` (serene), `nuloe` (content), `nemo` (grateful), `kuelo` (curious), `phaeli` (tender), `hurao` (patient), `shaelo` (brave), `phena` (kind), `thoru` (proud). A feeling in general is `phaelo`'s own event noun.

**The carves are done.** The synonym audit carved the joy pair (felt joy versus joy-giving character), the peace family (`shea` the noun, `phaelu` the abiding disposition, `shena` the stillness of the moment, `noalu` the deeper settled form, with `thiro` and `nuloe` adjacent), the thanks pair (`whaline` the expression, `woraka` the felt appreciation), and awe (`nelu`, the inward form, against `keluro`, the outward honor).

**The complex emotions already compose, one ruling short of a pattern.** The registry carries `korua thero` (anger, and hatred with it, per the Metta Sutta's own handling), `thiku nila` (contempt), `phelu pula` (envy, whose row carries the aside "no root is coined for this feeling"), and canon composes loneliness as `sonu` plus `nuhe`. Wonder is a question held as a feeling, `wela … welo` inside `phaelo`, attested in ch20 (`mia wela lopia nulae welo phaelo.`). The envy row's aside was the stance; nothing generalized it, so nothing prevented a future "guilt" or "anxiety" root from hiding a mechanism the registry pattern would have laid open.

**Corpus: the workhorses live, the rest sleep.** `lothea` 58 files, `shea` 50, `siora` 36 (inflated by its use as a register name); asleep at survey time: `nupira` 0, `kuelo` 0, `woraka` 0, `soliru` 0, `noalu` 0, `loshi` 1, `theomi` 1.

**One survey correction.** The session survey that queued this sweep listed "shewa" as an asleep emotion word; no such word exists in the lexicon. The error is recorded here so it does not propagate.
