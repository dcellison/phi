# Learning Phi: A Beginner's Tutorial

Welcome to Phi! This guide is designed to teach you the fundamentals of this beautiful, mindful language in a clear, step-by-step way.

Unlike a technical grammar, this tutorial will introduce concepts one at a time, building on what you've learned. We will use examples and simple explanations to help you understand not just *how* the language works, but *why* it works the way it does.

## How to Read This Guide

Each example in Phi will be presented in a three-line format, which is a standard way linguists analyze languages.

1.  **The Phi Sentence:** The sentence as it is written in Phi.
    *   `mia nima.`
2.  **The Gloss:** A word-for-word, literal translation with grammatical information in SMALL CAPS.
    *   `1SG sleep.`
3.  **The Translation:** The natural, idiomatic English translation.
    *   I sleep.

Understanding the gloss line is the key to seeing the underlying structure of the language.
