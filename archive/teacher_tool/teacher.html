Type a sentence the primer's household might say, and watch it become Phi — the sentence, its gloss, its Tengwar, and an explanation of every step. This page runs on rules, not on a model: it demonstrates the taught patterns, exactly as the primer teaches them, and it is honest about what lies beyond them. Transmutation itself — the rebuilding of an idea from Phi's own concepts — is judgment, and stays human; this teacher shows you the grammar that judgment works with.
