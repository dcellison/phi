# lothea

`[lo̞θe̞ä]`

english: *love*

`lothea` can be used as either a noun or a verb. as a noun it is abstract, which means that it isn't tangible or touchable. `lothea` is used as a noun in this sentence: *i knew the feeling in my heart was love*. the word *lothea* can also be used as a verb: 

phi: mia thi lothea
english: i love you
